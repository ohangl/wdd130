FAMILY RAFTING ADVENTURE

Perfect for families and first-timers! Our Family Rafting Adventure offers gentle rapids and majestic scenery. Float down the serene river and experience the natural beauty of the landscape. Children as young as six can participate, making this a great way to introduce your family to the joys of rafting.

WHITEWATER CHALLENGE

For adrenaline junkies, the Whitewater Challenge is a high-intensity, full-day expedition. Navigate through Class IV and V rapids under the guidance of our expert instructors. This trip is not for the faint-hearted but promises an unforgettable thrill. Participants must be at least 16 years old and should have prior rafting experience.

SUNSET PADDLE

Immerse yourself in nature with our multi-day Wilderness Expedition. This trip includes camping under the stars, fishing opportunities, and of course, exhilarating rapids. Ideal for those who want a complete outdoor experience. Participants should have some prior rafting experience and be comfortable with outdoor living.
